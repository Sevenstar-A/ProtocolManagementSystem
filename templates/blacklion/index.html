About our Mission and Vision

Our Mission

The CHS has the mission, within a scholarly environment, of providing high quality and regionally relevant health sciences training, research and community services at both undergraduate and postgraduate levels. It is engaged in producing ethical, knowledgeable, competent, and caring healthcare professionals who will become leaders in their respective disciplines.

Our Vision

The College of Health Sciences envisions to become a prominent African Health Sciences University dedicated to excellence in teaching, research and community health services.
